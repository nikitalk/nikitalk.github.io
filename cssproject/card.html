Giant Panda

The giant panda also known as panda bear or simply panda.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Weight: From 100 to 115 kg
  • Diet: Bamboo
  • Habitat: China

It is easily recognized by the large, distinctive black patches around its eyes, over the ears, and across its round body. Though it belongs to the order Carnivora, the giant panda's diet is over 99% bamboo.
